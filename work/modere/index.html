Context & Stakes

Modere was a global health & beauty brand trying to double past $500M in revenue on top of a brittle, home-grown platform. The company was running a billion-dollar direct sales operation on legacy tech that was expensive to maintain, hard to change, and brittle under scale. Leadership was blunt: they didn't believe they could break the $500M ceiling without a complete rethink.

March 2023: Discovery & Kickoff

Assessed legacy platform, mapped MLM business rules, defined composable architecture strategy and vendor selection.

June 2023: Architecture Defined

Locked in BigCommerce (headless), Pimcore (PIM), Contentstack (CMS), and custom promotion engine. Aligned leadership and vendors around composable vision.

November 2023: Core Platform Build

Headless commerce, PIM, and CMS integrated. Affiliate flows, shared cart pattern, and payment integrations (Braintree, Klarna, PayQuicker) in place.

March 2024: Mobile & Market Integration

MobiLoud mobile app wrapped and localized for 10 markets and 7 languages. Single composable stack serving web and app.

June 2024: Full Stack Launch

Complete platform live. 43% conversion uplift, 35% faster page loads. Architecture later nominated for 2025 MACH Impact Award.
