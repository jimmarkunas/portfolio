Opening Report: Homes in Darkness

Local news coverage that kicked off the program

Residents on San Antonio's Northeast Side went to local TV because whole blocks were in the dark for weeks. Nobody knew who owned which pole, how to get a repair scheduled, or why calls to CPS kept disappearing into a black hole.

City Council members were getting emails and phone calls every day about safety, fear, and kids walking home on unlit streets. The narrative was simple and brutal: San Antonio can't keep the lights on.

"For us, this is more than just a streetlight program‚Äîit's about creating a safer, greener, and more connected San Antonio. By embracing smart technologies, we are enhancing public safety, improving energy efficiency, and paving the way for a more sustainable future."

‚Äî Richard Medina, Chief Energy Delivery Officer at CPS Energy

Challenge Map

San Antonio Streetlight Complexity

A citywide outage problem with fragmented handoffs across residents, support, dispatch, field crews, and reporting systems. The challenge was not a single broken tool, but a broken operating chain.

Multiple intake paths and inconsistent resident data
Disconnected operational systems and manual handoffs
Bottlenecks created long repair delays and poor visibility

Systems & Data Blockers

  • No single system of record across CPS, Dalkia, City
  • Residents lacked clear reporting path
  • Support couldn't see unified status
  • No trustworthy metrics for leadership

Operations Blockers

  • Field dispatch via email/spreadsheets
  • Manual workflows = 3+ week delays
  • No visibility into workload or progress
  • Every repair was bespoke

Success Criteria: Single resident flow. Shared CPS/Dalkia view. 1‚Äì4 day repair SLA. Metrics for city leadership on safety/carbon/equity.

What I Owned

Product Requirements & KPIs

Turned public complaints and political pressure into product requirements and measurable KPIs

Stakeholder Alignment

Aligned CPS, Dalkia, and city stakeholders to a shared operational view and shared numbers

User Journey Definition

Defined the user journeys for residents, call center staff, field crews, and executives

Delivery & Integration

Drove delivery of workflows, dashboards, and integrations (including SAP)

Key Constraint: CPS is a municipal utility. Dalkia is an international contractor. The city has its own incentives. I got all three to use one shared platform and agree on the numbers, without a startup's level of control or budget.

Product Vision: Boringly Reliable & Transparently Green Lights

The product vision was to make streetlights feel like a modern utility service instead of a mystery. Residents should have one simple place to report a problem and see that it is being fixed. CPS and Dalkia should share a single operational view that ties outages, work orders, and budget together.
