Context & Stakes

When Dov Charney was pushed out, American Apparel was a vertically-integrated icon with LA-made basics, 260 stores in 20 countries, and a reputation for chaos. The interim CEO gave Amanda a blunt mandate: if digital and omni-channel didn't move the needle fast, there might not be a company left to save. eCommerce ran on a custom .NET stack bolted to an old Microsoft Dynamics instance. Each of the 16 regional sites had its own templates, currencies, and hacks. Inventory was a black box.

Board Mandate — June 2014

Dov Charney pushed out. Interim CEO gives Amanda a blunt directive: double revenue and make omni-channel real before Wall Street loses patience. Amanda brings me in as technical co-pilot to turn ambition into a working system.

Oracle ATG Global Re-Platform

16 regional .NET/Dynamics sites moved onto a single Oracle ATG stack. Shared IA, UX, and catalog rules across all markets. Mulesoft wired between ATG and the back office — ERP, POS, and OMS all seeing the same orders, prices, and promotions. Launched a new country every 12 hours during cutover, sleeping at the LA factory.

RFID Inventory Spine

Item-level RFID deployed in the LA factory and 200+ stores. Every SKU locatable by store, size, and color in real time. Web and store associates seeing the same stock picture for the first time. Manual cycle counts eliminated — 20–30 staff hours saved per store per week.

BOPIS, Ship-to-Store & Upsell Engine

Order routing engine live — weighing inventory, distance, shipping cost, and delivery promise for every order. BOPIS and ship-to-store go from zero to meaningful revenue share. Upsell engine surfaces in-stock local store items before checkout, turning web orders into higher-margin store-anchored sales.

Q1 Revenue Results

CEO Paula Schneider connects Q1 revenue gains directly to the omni-channel program: "You two went to bat for the company. Q1 revenue is up significantly. Keep it up, we may climb out of this mess yet." eCommerce up 38% — then 37% again six months later. Store sales up 14%. Shrink down 65%.

Blueprint Established — June 2015

The AA stack — ATG, RFID, Mulesoft, routing logic, upsell engine — documented as a reusable omni-channel blueprint. Team members went on to replicate similar patterns at other brands. What we invented as we went became the playbook everyone else followed.
