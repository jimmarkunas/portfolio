Context & Stakes

Mrs. Meyer's was a cult-favorite brand with zero direct channel. SC Johnson depended entirely on Target, Walmart, and other retailers for both sales and customer insight. There was no owned relationship with end customers, no way to test bundles or subscriptions, and no first-party data to inform marketing or product decisions. Operationally, everything was tuned for pallets, not parcels ‚Äî SAP and the LiveArea/Merkle WMS were built to ship truckloads to retailers, not thousands of individual consumer orders.
